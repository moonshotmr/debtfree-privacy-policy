# Privacy Policy for DebtFree

Last updated: June 2026

DebtFree is a personal debt tracking and payoff planning app. This policy explains what information the app handles and how it is used.

## Information You Provide

DebtFree stores the information you enter into the app only on the device, including:

- Your name, optional email address, and optional profile photo
- Debt names, balances, interest rates, due dates, notes, and debt categories
- Payment history that you record
- App preferences such as payoff method, budget, reminders, appearance, and achievement settings

## How Information Is Used

This information is used only to provide app features, including debt tracking, payoff calculations, reminders, charts, exports, iCloud backup, and Debt Coach guidance.

DebtFree does not sell your personal information, does not track your location, and does not share your debt information with advertising networks or data brokers. All information stays entirely on user(your) device or user's icloud.

## Data Storage

DebtFree stores your app data locally on your device using Apple-provided storage. If you use the premium iCloud backup feature, your debt data, payment history, preferences, profile information, and custom debt ordering may also be stored in your personal iCloud account through Apple iCloud services.

Subscription purchases and payment processing are handled by Apple through the App Store. DebtFree does not receive your credit card number or Apple ID payment details.

## Photos

If you choose to add a profile photo, DebtFree uses Apple photo picker controls so you can select an image. The selected image is stored as part of your local app profile data and, if you choose to use iCloud backup, may be included in your iCloud-backed app data.

## Notifications

If you allow notifications, DebtFree schedules local payment reminders on your device. Notification permission can be changed in iOS Settings at any time.

## Data Deletion

You can delete app data from DebtFree by using Settings > Clear All Data or by uninstalling the app. If you have used iCloud backup, you can also manage or delete iCloud app data from your device's iCloud settings.

## Children's Privacy

DebtFree is not directed to children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes

This policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version should be posted at the public privacy policy URL used in App Store Connect and inside the app.
